Published Work

The Role of Trust in Technology Adoption: Do Perceived Ease of Use and Usefulness Drive Cloud Computing Acceptance of Micro Enterprises in the Philippines?

I am one of the co-authors of the research paper “The Role of Trust in Technology Adoption: Do Perceived Ease of Use and Usefulness Drive Cloud Computing Acceptance of Micro Enterprises in the Philippines?” presented at the 16th International Conference on E-Education, E-Business, E-Management and E-Learning (IC4e 2025) and published by IEEE. The study explores how trust, perceived ease of use, and perceived usefulness influence cloud computing adoption among micro enterprises, offering insights into how small businesses embrace technology to drive growth and innovation.
